About TendUP

TendUP continues Yuzu. Yuzu was an app that provided us with the opportunity to emulate Nintendo Switch games. They were sued for 2.4 million dollars because people used it to pirate games. This was never Yuzu's intention to let this happen. The same goes for us. We do not support piracy and we do not cover it. More information about piracy can be found in our terms of service.
